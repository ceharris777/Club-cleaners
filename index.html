What We Offer

Expert Services for Every Club in Your Bag

From a basic detail to a complete restoration, we have a solution that fits your clubs and your budget.

01 🧹

Club Cleaning

Our comprehensive cleaning process removes embedded dirt, grass, and grime from every surface — faces, hosels, ferrules, and grips — restoring both appearance and feel.

  • Full face and groove cleaning
  • Hosel and shaft wipe-down
  • Restores original shine
  • Improves contact consistency
02 🔧

Groove Sharpening

Worn grooves are the #1 reason golfers lose spin and shot control around the greens. Our sharpening process restores factory-level groove depth for maximum ball interaction.

  • Restores factory groove geometry
  • Maximizes backspin on approach shots
  • Better control in wet conditions
  • Legal within USGA specifications
03 ⚗️

Rust Removal

Rust doesn't just look bad — it degrades performance and accelerates further deterioration. Our treatment safely removes oxidation without damaging the underlying steel or finish.

  • Safe chemical & mechanical removal
  • Prevents further corrosion spread
  • Restores structural integrity
  • Pre-treatment before polishing
04 ✨

Buffing & Polishing

The finishing touch. Our multi-stage polishing process produces a mirror-quality finish that makes your clubs look better than they did leaving the factory.

  • Multi-stage compound polishing
  • Satin or mirror finish options
  • UV-resistant protective coat
  • Long-lasting luster
05 🖐️

Grip Replacement & Shaft Work

A worn grip costs you control; the wrong shaft length costs you distance. We replace grips with premium rubber, cord, or wrap options, and offer precision shaft shortening and lengthening so every club fits your stance and swing perfectly. Pricing varies based on club type, grip brand, and shaft specifications — contact us for a quote.

  • Wide selection of grip styles and materials
  • Shaft shortening for better control & accuracy
  • Shaft lengthening for added reach and distance
  • Matched to your height, posture, and swing
  • Priced per club based on specifications
